Descripción

Lost Ark is a free-to-play, massively multiplayer online action role-playing adventure game, developed by Smilegate RPG. The game takes players on a journey in a world that has descended into chaos to find the fabled shards of the Lost Ark. Players explore the wilds of Arkesia, from sailing beautiful oceans to delving into dark dungeons, customize their buildout in a variety of classes, including unique skill combinations, skill progression, and their personal style. Lost Ark spans a massive world brimming with dark and wondrous fantasy, filled with secrets for players to discover alone or with friends.
