Descripción

Tarisland, a new MMORPG, brings back the fun of challenging dungeons and experiencing immersive adventures in a classic fantasy art style. Choose from 9 classes and 18 specializations to create your build. Team up globally to conquer epic bosses!

Explore the Vast World
Dive into the vast realm of Tarisland, uncover hidden mysteries, and embark on your unique adventure.


Class & Talent System
Tarisland offers 9 unique classes, each with 2 specializations for you to choose from.


Create Your Own Build
Each class features a flexible and extensive talent tree, easily customizable for different playstyles.


Challenge Various Dungeons & Raids
Breathe new life into the classic raid and dungeon system, team up to take down epic bosses!


Enjoy the Game on Steam & Mobile
Available on both Steam and mobile with data-sharing enabled. Challenge dungeons and raids anytime, anywhere.


Trade in the Auction House
Earn gold coins by trading in the Auction House! Collecting, mining, crafting, and engaging in other professions are other ways to earn rewards!
